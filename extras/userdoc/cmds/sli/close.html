Name:
close - close a stream
Parameters: 
stream(istreamtype|ostreamtype): The stream to close
Author: 
R Kupper
Synopsis: 
stream close -> -
Remarks: 
This is just a trie-wrapper for "closeistream" and "closeostream".

Description: 
This function closes a stream. This may be an istream  
                or an ostream.
